## ✨ Implemented AI Features

### 1. **🛡️ Buyer Trust Score and Ratings**
**Location**: AI Features Screen → Trust Score Tab (Buyers)

**What it does**:
- Calculates trust scores (0-100) for buyers using AI algorithms
- Factors considered:
  - Order history completion rate
  - Payment reliability
  - Communication response time
  - Account age and standing
- Awards badges: Verified Buyer, Reliable Payment, Zero Disputes, Quick Responder, Trusted Member
- Risk assessment: Low, Medium, High
- Visual trust indicators with progress bars

**How to access**:
- Buyers: Click "AI Intelligence Hub" card → Trust Score tab
- Farmers: View buyer trust scores in order requests

---

### 2. **📊 Crop Demand Prediction**
**Location**: AI Features Screen → Demand Tab

**What it does**:
- Predicts market demand for different crops (0-100 score)
- AI analyzes:
  - Seasonal patterns
  - Regional market trends
  - Festival seasons
  - Historical demand data
- Shows demand levels: Very High, High, Moderate, Low
- Trend indicators: Rising 📈, Stable →, Falling 📉
- Predicted price per kg
- 75-95% AI confidence scores
- Detailed insights and reasons for predictions

**How to access**:
- Click "AI Intelligence Hub" → Demand tab
- Select any crop (rice, wheat, cotton, tomato, etc.)

---

### 3. **💰 Smart Price Suggestion Engine**
**Location**: AI Features Screen → Pricing Tab (Farmers only)

**What it does**:
- AI-powered optimal pricing recommendations
- Provides: Minimum, Recommended (best value), Maximum prices
- Considers:
  - Crop grade (premium, grade-a, etc.)
  - Quantity (bulk discounts)
  - Freshness (harvest date)
  - Regional demand
  - Market averages
- Shows competitor analysis
- 80-95% confidence ratings
- Detailed pricing factors breakdown

**How to access**:
- Farmers: Click "AI Intelligence Hub" → Pricing tab

---

### 4. **📈 Smart Sale Adviser**
**Location**: AI Features Screen → Adviser Tab (Farmers only)

**What it does**:
- AI generates personalized selling strategies
- Provides:
  - Best time to sell recommendations
  - Expected revenue projections
  - Market condition assessment (Favorable/Moderate/Challenging)
  - Actionable recommendations
  - Market opportunities identification
  - Risk factor warnings
- Real-time market insights
- Data-driven selling tactics

**How to access**:
- Farmers: Click "AI Intelligence Hub" → Adviser tab

---

### 5. **🤖 AI Chatbot Assistant**
**Location**: Floating button (bottom-right) on all authenticated screens

**What it does**:
- 24/7 AI-powered support assistant
- Answers FAQs about:
  - Registration & KYC
  - Orders & Payments
  - Pricing strategies
  - Market insights
  - Platform features
- Context-aware responses (farmer vs buyer)
- Quick question shortcuts
- Natural language processing
- Real-time chat with typing indicators

**How to access**:
- Click the floating AI bot button (purple gradient) on bottom-right
- Available on all screens when logged in

**Try these questions**:
- "How to register?"
- "What is Trust Score?"
- "How to place order?"
- "Pricing help"
- "KYC verification"

---

### 6. **📅 Seasonal Crop Calendar Assistance**
**Location**: AI Features Screen → Calendar Tab

**What it does**:
- AI-powered planting & harvest guide
- Season-specific crop recommendations
- For each recommended crop:
  - Optimal planting window
  - Expected harvest window
  - Yield predictions per acre
  - Market demand assessment (High/Medium/Low)
  - Profit potential percentage
- Regional customization (based on user's state)
- Current season highlighting

**How to access**:
- Click "AI Intelligence Hub" → Calendar tab
